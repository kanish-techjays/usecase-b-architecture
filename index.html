What is Use Case B?

Every service visit ends with paperwork: the technician has to describe what was wrong, what they did, what parts they used, and whether the customer's compliance rules were followed. Today that write-up is typed by hand, its quality depends entirely on who is typing, and mistakes are only discovered days later in the office, when the fix is a phone call or a repeat truck roll. Use Case B puts an AI copilot inside the technician's mobile app that does three things: it briefs the technician on the site's history before they arrive, it drafts the job write-up from what they entered in the app, and it checks the ticket for missing or vague information before it can be submitted, so problems are caught on site, not days later.
